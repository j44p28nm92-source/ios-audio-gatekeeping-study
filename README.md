# ios-audio-gatekeeping-study

#### Background: A problem arises when an increasing number of mechanisms disrupt the free market for niche products (in this case, the iOS audio market) in a variety of ways. This ranges from (more or less) incomplete listings to active market manipulation by self-proclaimed experts and questionable, self-serving communities – but also by individual players who profit from the chaos.
